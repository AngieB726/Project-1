The Ultimate Weekend for Pet Lovers

Super Pet Expo is a high-energy weekend designed for pet lovers of all kinds, bringing together shopping, live shows, rescue organizations, and hands-on experiences in one exciting space. From discovering new pet products to meeting adoptable animals and watching interactive demos, there is something for dogs, cats, and everyone in between. Bring the whole family, connect with fellow animal lovers, and enjoy a fun-filled event that celebrates pets and the people who care for them.

What is a Super Pet Expo?

The Super Pet Expo is a premier, family-friendly 3-day event (Jan 9–11, 2026, in Edison, NJ) featuring over 150 vendors, educational seminars from pet experts, and entertainment like the Doggie Derby, cat agility shows, and reptile exhibits. It is a massive, interactive shopping extravaganza for pet lovers to explore products, meet animals, and socialize with their pets in a fun, lively atmosphere.
